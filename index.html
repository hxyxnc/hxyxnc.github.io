Nachuan Xiao

I am a post-doctoral fellow in the Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA), National University of Singapore. I got my Ph.D. degree in the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Ya-xiang Yuan. I got my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Fudan University in 2016.

My email: xnc@lsec.cc.ac.cn

Research Interests

  • Nonlinear optimization

  • Optimization problems on Riemannian manifolds

  • Nonsmooth optimization

My research interests include numerical methods for optimization problems with orthogonality constraints, especially in developing orthonormalization-free approaches for these problems. I am also interested in nonsmooth optimization problems with orthogonality constraints and their applications arisen from statistics and computer vision.
